StudyQuestz - Gamifying Learning

An Innovative Approach to Learning

StudyQuestz transforms the traditional study process into an engaging game, making learning both effective and fun. By gamifying the experience of studying, this app aims to boost motivation and enhance knowledge retention for students of all ages.

Key Features

  • Interactive flashcard games
  • Levels and rewards to encourage progress
  • Customizable study sets for personalized learning

Technology Stack

  • Developed using Python
  • Randomization for flashcard shuffling
  • User-friendly interface design
